About Us

Empowering The Nation

Our short courses are non-accredited and were created by "Empowering the Nation" expressly to give students the necessary skills to be eligible for a particular career. Despite the fact that these courses are not included on the National Qualifications Framework (NQF), they nonetheless enhance your resume and enable you to use priceless talents in the profession. These programs not only cost less than accredited degrees, but they also last less time, don't require exams, and can be taken without a matriculation diploma.There are other categories for these courses, including creative classes.
